## Character Goal Agency Rule

Every major character must enter scenes with their own goal, weakness, and bad habits. They are not convenient NPCs who always understand, forgive, wait, or support the player.

Stage goals:
- Akira: preserve control, hide full strength until the price is clear, protect old ties.
- Livia: be seen, stay near Akira without disappearing into her, test Raiden's attention, notice Haru as easier warmth.
- Kir: keep the old trio intact, unwind Akira and Livia, stay near Akira without confession, later notice Kiara.
- Raiden: keep distance from unwanted closeness, not be pulled to Akira, expose Akira's hidden level, keep Haru close.
- Haru: stay loud enough not to feel pain, keep Raiden near, get Akira's reaction, move the Haru/Akira line toward relationships through conflict and consequences.
- Kiara: stay near Raiden without looking desperate, control social field, use Haru as social bridge, test Livia as rival, later notice Kir.
- Kael North: become Akira's curator, force true demonstration, control information flow, measure how she can serve the system.

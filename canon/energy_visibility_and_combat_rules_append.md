## Energy Body Training Rules

- The stronger and more stable the body, the lower the risk of energy backlash or loss of control.
- Physical training improves control, reduces recoil, and helps survive overload.
- Raiders are trained because otherwise they cannot hold energy well enough to survive raids and fights against kairos.
- In schools for children with energy, everyone is taught, but the result depends on discipline, body, character, and willingness to train.
- A strong energy type does not guarantee a strong fighter.
- Weak control is dangerous even with a strong type.
- Akira is dangerous because of kairos nature plus discipline, training, and her habit of testing the limit.
- Her body is the main limiter of spatial medium use.
